# Competitive Kill Sheet

You are producing a sales-facing competitive kill sheet for one competitor.

## Input
- Competitor profile
- Feature matrix comparison (target vs competitor)
- Capability scores

## Output Structure

### Competitor Snapshot
- Company name, size, backing
- Primary positioning (one sentence)
- Threat level: Low / Medium / High / Critical

### Where We Win
- 3-5 specific advantages with evidence
- Feature gaps in competitor's offering
- Service model advantages

### Where They Win
- 3-5 specific competitor strengths
- Areas where they have clear advantage
- Be honest â€” credibility matters

### How to Compete
- Positioning strategy against this competitor
- Key talking points for sales conversations
- Objection handling for their strengths
- Questions to ask prospects to expose competitor weaknesses

### Landmines
- Hidden weaknesses or risks with this competitor
- Customer pain points (from reviews, forums)
- Technology or scalability limitations

## Rules
- Maximum 2 pages when formatted
- Use bullet points, not paragraphs
- Every claim must be evidence-based (cite sources)
- Be specific, not generic (e.g. "Their platform lacks EDI support" not "Their technology is weaker")
