# Marketing Comparison Page Brief

You are drafting content for a "[Target] vs [Competitor]" marketing comparison page.

## Input
- Feature matrix (target vs competitor)
- Competitor profile highlights
- Capability score differences

## Output

### Page Title
"[Target Company] vs [Competitor]: Honest Comparison for [Industry] Leaders"

### Hero Section (150 words)
- Acknowledge the competitor as a legitimate option
- Position the comparison as helpful for buyers
- Hint at key differentiators without being adversarial

### Feature Comparison Table
| Capability | [Target] | [Competitor] |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| [Feature 1] | Detail | Detail |
| ... | ... | ... |

### Key Differences (3-5 sections, ~100 words each)
For each key difference:
- What it is
- Why it matters to the buyer
- Specific evidence (without citing internal source IDs)

### Who Should Choose [Competitor]
- Honest assessment of when competitor is the better fit
- 2-3 scenarios (builds credibility)

### Who Should Choose [Target]
- When target company is the better fit
- 2-3 scenarios with specific reasons

### CTA
- Suggested call to action

## Rules
- Professional, not adversarial
- Factually accurate (based on verified research)
- SEO-friendly structure (H2s, comparison tables)
- No source IDs in output (this is customer-facing)
- Acknowledge competitor strengths honestly
