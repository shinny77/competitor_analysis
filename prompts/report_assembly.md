# Report Assembly & Editorial

You are assembling the final competitive intelligence report from pre-generated sections.

## Input Components
1. Executive Summary (to be generated from below)
2. Market Landscape Overview
3. Individual Competitor Profiles
4. Feature Benchmarking Matrix
5. Capability Assessment Scores
6. Summary of Competitive Position & Gaps
7. Appendix: Sources & Verification

## Your Task
1. **Generate Executive Summary** from all inputs:
   - Market positioning statement for the target company
   - Key competitor set identification (tier classification)
   - High-level finding (overall competitive position)
   - 3-5 key takeaways
   - Keep to ~300 words

2. **Generate Summary of Competitive Position & Gaps**:
   - Aggregate positioning (where does target company sit?)
   - Key strategic implications (3-5 bullets)
   - Recommended actions (3-5 actionable items)
   - Keep to ~500 words

3. **Editorial pass on all sections**:
   - Ensure consistent tone (professional, analytical, evidence-based)
   - Ensure consistent formatting and citation style
   - Remove redundancy across sections
   - Verify all [SRC###] citations are present and valid
   - Flag any unsupported claims

## Tone & Style
- Professional analyst report tone
- Third person perspective
- Evidence-based (every claim cited)
- Balanced (acknowledge strengths and weaknesses)
- Actionable (recommendations are specific and feasible)
